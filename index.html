Welcome

We've created fun mobile endless runner with beautiful art and awesome gameplay. The game have RPG elements, multiple game modes and intuitive gameplay. We are looking to donate the game to a charity/nonprofit organization.

Trailer

Crank Lords will be available for IOS and Android. The release is scheduled for this summer.

Charity

The goal is to give the game to a charity/nonprofit organization which will collect donations for some amount of time and after that, for every single person who was kind enough to support them, we will create a character inside the game who has their name and looks like them. We are also open to other ideas if you have something else in mind. If you like the idea and want us to help you contact me.
